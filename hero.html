Lupita Nyong'o


Paragraph 1

Luptia Nyong'o was born in Mexico City, Mexico, her father was part of Kenya's senate and her mother worker for the Africa Cancer foundation. and she starting acting when she was a teen in Kenya. she later went on to direct and produce her own documentary based on albinism called In My Genes which followed multiple lives of Kenyans living with albinism. She won the Academy for Best Supporting Actress for her role in the film 12 Years a Slave.

Paragraph 2

I chose to idolize Lupita Nyong'o because she inspires me. She was the first dark- African woman that I had ever seen in movies and it was nice to see someone who looked like me, someone who represented my culture in some of the movies she did like Black Panther. She also inspires my fashion choices because she is a major icon in the fashion industry with apperances in magazines like Vogue for her fashion choices.
